Median

A. Individual Series :

--> Odd Number of Observation
S. No. X Size of Ascending Order
1 10 9
2 15 10
3 9 15
4 25 19
5 19 20
\[Median = Size\ of \frac{(N + 1)^{th}}{2} Item\]
\[Median = Size\ of \frac{(5 + 1)^{th}}{2} = 3^{th}\ item\]
\[\boxed{3^{th}\ item\ is\ 15}\]
--> Even Number of Observation (N)
S. No. X Size of Ascending Order
1 10 9
2 15 10
3 9 15
4 25 19
\[Median\ = Size\ of\ \frac{(N + 1)^{th}}{2}\ Item\]
\[= Size\ of\ \frac{(4 + 1)^{th}}{2}\ = 2.5^{th} Item\]
\[= Size\ of\ \frac{2nd\ item + 3rd\ item}{2}\]
\[\boxed{= \frac{10 + 15}{2} = 12.5}\]

B. Median - Discrete Series

Size of shoes F c.f
5 10 10
5.5 16 26
6 28 54
6.5 15 69
7 30 99
7.5 40 139
8 34 173

c.f = Commulative Frequency

\[Median\ = Size\ of\ \frac{(N + 1)^{th}}{2}\ item\]
\[= Size\ of\ \frac{(173 + 1)^{th}}{2}\ item\]
\[= Size\ of\ 87^{th}\ item\]
\[\boxed{Median = 7}\]

C. Median - Continuous Series

x F c.f
10-25 6 6
25-40 20 26
40-55 44 70
55-70 26 96
70-85 3 99
85-100 1 100
\[Median = L+\frac{\frac{N}{2} - cf}{f} \times i\]

range = x > \(\frac{N}{2}\)

range = x > \(\frac{100}{2}\) = 50

range = x > 50

range = 40-55

L = Lower Limit of range = 40

cf = Previous Commulative Friquency = 26

f = frequency of median class = 44

i = interval size (width : 55 - 40) = 15

\[Median = 40 + \frac{\frac{100}{2} - 26}{44} \times 15\]
\[= 40 + \frac{50 - 26}{44} \times 15\]
\[= 40 + 8.18\]
\[\boxed{Median = 48 \ .18\ marks}\]
