ANOVA (Very Important)

2 नहीं बल्कि 3 या उससे ज्यादा groups हों तो ANOVA (Analysis of Variance) आता है।

Problem That ANOVA Solves

मान लो एक company ने 3 training programs बनाए:

  • Program A
  • Program B
  • Program C

और training के बाद scores मिले:

Group A Group B Group C
70 80 90
75 85 95
80 90 100

Question:

क्या तीनों programs का average score समान है?

क्या हम 3 t-tests लगा सकते हैं?

A vs B
A vs C
B vs C

❌ Technically कर सकते हैं, लेकिन इससे Type I Error बढ़ जाती है।

इसी समस्या को solve करने के लिए ANOVA बना।

ANOVA का Main Idea

ANOVA Mean को directly compare नहीं करता।

यह Variance compare करता है।

इसलिए नाम है:

Analysis of Variance

Hypotheses

Null Hypothesis
\[H_0 : \mu_1 = \mu_2 = \mu_3\]

सभी group means समान हैं।

Alternative Hypothesis
\[H_0 :\]

कम से कम एक mean अलग है।

ANOVA का Secret Question

ANOVA पूछता है:

Groups के बीच का variation ज्यादा है?

या

Groups के अंदर का variation ज्यादा है?

If Between-Group Variation is Large

Means अलग हैं।

Reject \(H_0\)

If Within-Group Variation is Large

Difference random chance हो सकता है।

Fail to Reject \(H_0\)

F-Test Concept

ANOVA का test statistic:

जहाँ:

  • \(MS_{between}\) = Between Groups Mean Square
  • \(MS_{within}\) = Within Groups Mean Square

Intuition

अगर:

\[F \approx 1\]

तो groups लगभग समान हैं।

अगर:

\[F \gg 1\]

तो groups अलग हो सकते हैं।

ANOVA Roadmap

पहले हम समझेंगे:

  1. Group Mean
  2. Grand mean
  3. Between Group variance (SSB - sum of Square between)
  4. Within Group variance (SSW - sum of Square within)
  5. F statistic
  6. Critical Value
  7. P-Value
  8. Final Conclusion

🎯 जैसे Chi-Square में पूरा chapter एक formula के आसपास घूमता था:

\[\chi^2 = \sum \frac{(O - E)^2}{E}\]

वैसे ही ANOVA पूरा इस idea के आसपास घूमता है:

\[F = \frac{Between\ Group\ variance}{Within\ Group\ variance}\]

One-Way ANOVA — Complete Numerical Example

Situation

एक company ने 3 training programs की effectiveness compare की।

Training के बाद test scores:

Program A Program B Program C
70 80 90
75 85 95
80 90 100

Step 1: Hypotheses

Null Hypothesis

\[H_0 : \mu_1 = \mu_2 \mu_3\]

Alternative Hypothesis

\[H_1\]

कम से कम एक mean अलग है।

Step 2: Group Means

Program A

\[\bar{X}_A = \frac{70 \times 75 \times 80}{3}\]
\[\boxed{\bar{X} = 75}\]

Program B

\[\bar{X}_B = \frac{80 \times 85 \times 90}{3}\]
\[\boxed{\bar{X}_B = 85}\]

Program C

\[\bar{X}_C = \frac{90 \times 95 \times 100}{3}\]
\[\boxed{\bar{X}_C = 95}\]

Step 3: Grand Mean

Grand Mean = सभी observations का mean

\[GM = \frac{70+75+80+80+85+90+90+95+100}{9}\]
\[GM = \frac{765}{9} = 85\]

✅ Grand Mean = 85

Step 4: Between Group Variation (SSB)

Sum of Square between

यह measure करता है:

Group Means Grand Mean से कितनी दूर हैं।

Formula:

\[SSB = \sum n_i(\bar{X}_i - GM)^2\]

Program A

\[SSB = 3(75 - 85)^2\]
\[\boxed{SSB = 300}\]

Program B

\[SSB = 3(85 - 85)^2\]
\[\boxed{SSB = 0}\]

Program C

\[SSB = 3(95 - 85)^2\]
\[\boxed{SSB = 300}\]

Total:

\[SSB = 300 + 0 + 300\]
\[\boxed{SSB = 600}\]

✅ SSB = 600

Step 5: Within Group Variation (SSW)

sum of Square within

यह measure करता है:

Group के अंदर observations mean से कितनी दूर हैं।

Formula:

\[SSW = \sum (X - \bar{X}_i)^2\]

Program A

Mean = 75

\[SSW = (70 - 75)^2 + (75 - 75)^2 + (80 - 75)^2\]
\[SSW = 25 + 0 + 25\]
\[\boxed{SSW = 50}\]

Program B

Mean = 85

\[SSW = (80 - 85)^2 + (85 - 85)^2 + (90 - 85)^2\]
\[SSW = 25 + 0 + 25\]
\[\boxed{SSW = 50}\]

Program C

Mean = 95

\[SSW = (90 - 95)^2 + (95 - 95)^2 + (100 - 95)^2\]
\[SSW = 25 + 0 + 25\]
\[\boxed{SSW = 50}\]

Total :

\[SSW = 150\]

✅ SSW = 150

Step 6: Degrees of Freedom

Between Groups

\[df_B = k - 1\]
\[df_B = 3 - 1\]
\[\boxed{df_B = 2}\]

Within Groups

\[df_W = N - K\]
\[df_W = 9 - 3\]
\[\boxed{df_W = 6}\]

Step 7: Mean Squares

MSB

Mean Square Between

\[MSB = \frac{SSB}{df_B}\]
\[MSB = \frac{600}{2}\]
\[\boxed{MSB = 300}\]

MSW

Mean Square Within

\[MSB = \frac{SSW}{df_W}\]
\[MSW = \frac{150}{6}\]
\[\boxed{MSB = 25}\]

Step 8: F Statistic

\[F = \frac{Between\ Group\ variance}{Within\ Group\ variance}\]
\[F = \frac{300}{25}\]
\[\boxed{F = 12}\]

✅ F = 12

Step 9: Critical Value Decision

Depends on:

  • \(\alpha\)
  • \(df_B\)
  • \(df_W\)

for :

\[\alpha = 0.05\]
\[df_B = 2\]
\[df_W = 6\]

Critical Value \(\approx\)

\[5.14\]

Compare:

\[12>5.14\]

✅ Reject \(H_0\)

Step 10: p-value Decision

Depends on:

  • \(F\ static\)
  • \(df_B\)
  • \(df_W\)

for :

\[F = 12\]
\[df_B = 2\]
\[df_w = 6\]

P-Value \(\approx\)

\[0.008\]

Compare:

\[0.008 < 0.05\]

✅ Reject \(H_0\)

Final Conclusion

Since

\[F=12>5.14\]

and

\[p < 0.05\]

we reject \(H_0\)

Therefore:

There is sufficient evidence to conclude that at least one training program has a different mean score.

🎯 ANOVA में याद रखने वाली सबसे बड़ी बात:

  • SSB बड़ा ⇒ Groups के means दूर-दूर हैं।
  • SSW बड़ा ⇒ Groups के अंदर बहुत variation है।
  • F बड़ा ⇒ Means अलग होने का evidence मजबूत है।
